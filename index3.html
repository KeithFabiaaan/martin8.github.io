Boxing is a combat sport in which two competitors face each other in a ring and throw punches with gloved hands


Score more points through clean punches or win by knockout (KO), technical knockout (TKO), or judges' decision.

Jab, cross, hook, uppercut, footwork, head movement, and defensive maneuvers like slipping, blocking, and parrying..
